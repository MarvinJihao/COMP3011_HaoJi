External API:

  API1: NASA EONET 环境灾害事件
  doc: https://eonet.gsfc.nasa.gov/docs/v3
  https://eonet.gsfc.nasa.gov/how-to-guide
    demo展示顺序: 
      1. preview external data: GET /ingest/eonet/wildfires/preview
      2. ingest 数据:  POST /ingest/eonet/wildfires/sync
      3. 查看数据库 GET /fire-events
      4. analytics: GET /analytics/summary / GET /analytics/timeseries/daily / GET /analytics/hotspots


  API2: USGS Earthquake API（地震）
  doc: https://earthquake.usgs.gov/fdsnws/event/1/


  API3: OpenWeatherMap（天气）
